How to Play:

Help the Kitty protect its treasures from the Ants!

  • Move Arrows Up/Down/Left/Right to move the Kitty
  • Hit any of the 4 ant bases to freeze the ants for 3 seconds and
    +30 points
  • Hit any of the Brown Ants to slow them down for 2 seconds.
    AVOID the Poisonous Red Ants
  • Press "h" key for Kitty to hiss and scare the ants to slow down for
    2 seconds. Only can be used 3 times in 1 game, -10 points per hiss
  • Maximum game time is 1 minute, +100 bonus points
